A/C SERVICE: FREON RECHARGE

The weather in Texas is unpredictable, so make sure your HVAC (heating, ventilation and air conditioning) system can handle the blazing summer heat, and the blistering cold when it hits those few times per year! Guaranteed Tire and Auto professional technicians will inspect the A/C Services components. We will also perform an outlet temperature analysis which is designed to determine cooling performance. Then we will check system pressure readings using our state-of-the-Art A/C Machine. When determined that there are no leaks or any other malfunctions, if needed then we will recharge the Freon. If there are other issues, we will recommend a correct diagnostic service to troubleshoot those area(s) of concern.

POPULAR QUESTIONS

  • Should I consider using synthetic blend oil?

    Synthetic blend motor oil is more effective than regular oil because it lasts longer and can withstand higher temperatures without breaking down. Synthetic motor oils can improve vehicle horsepower and efficiency because they produce less resistance in an engine than regular motor oils.

  • What parts should be replaced?

    We only recommend changing parts that are defective and if they cause a safety problem.

  • How do I keep track of routine?

    Check tire pressure regularly. Change winshield wipers if they don't clean properly. At least once a year have your car check out in a repair shop.
